anima sana

Amelia Simpson, BSc, MSc, PgD, is the founder of Amina Sana and is a Consultant Psychologist working in the UAE. Amelia is UK trained and Registered Psychologist with the HCPC (in the UK), CDA (in the UAE) and a Europe Certified EMDR therapist, registered with EMDR Europe and EMDRIA. Amelia is also highly experienced in other trauma informed therapeutic approaches such as, Compassion Focussed Therapy and Schema Therapy. Amelia is also a couples therapist working to support couples, using research-based methods to help couples compassionately manage their conflicts, deepen they friendship and intimacy and share their life purpose and dreams using the research based Gottman approach.

The Theory of EMDR

Dr. Francine Shapiro discovered EMDR in 1987 while walking in a park when she noticed that by moving her eyes rapidly back and forth while focusing on negative thoughts she could cause the disturbance to subside. At the time, she was a graduate student studying psychology and so she decided to research what was happening more closely. Out of her studies and test protocol emerged the therapy that we now know of as Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing. But how does EMDR actually work in the brain? We need to go into a bit more detail about the structure of the brain to fully understand what is happening.
